Core Trading Mandates

Agri-Softs & LATAM Origination

Focused on physical origination from Latin American hubs, providing end-to-end traceability and supply chain visibility for international buyers.

  • Cocoa & Coffee: Principal-led sourcing of cocoa beans (DR/Peru origin) and green coffee.
  • Grains & Pulses: Bulk volumes of corn, wheat, soy, and specialty pulses.
Compliance Note: All flows are strictly compliant with EUDR (European Union Deforestation Regulation) and international ESG standards.

Oils & Agri-Commodities

Management of high-volume bulk flows for food processing and industrial sectors, optimizing for logistical efficiency and price transparency.

  • Vegetable Oils: Crude/Refined Palm (CPO/CPKO), Soybean, Sunflower, Rapeseed, and Castor Oil.
  • Oilseeds & Meals: Protein meals and oilseeds for global feed and food supply chains.
Compliance Note: Real-time sanction screening and rigorous counterparty verification applied to all origin-to-destination routes.

Energy & Industrial Minerals

Execution of large-scale bulk commodity transfers for the marine and energy sectors, emphasizing reliable delivery and institutional contract terms.

  • Energy: Marine and Industrial Fuel Oils, Thermal Hard Coal.
Compliance Note: Operations conducted under strict OFAC/OFSI screening protocols within G7-compliant corridors.

Fertilizers

Essential agricultural inputs managed through secure, principal-led supply chains to support global farming productivity.

  • Nitrogen-based: Urea, Ammonia, and NPK blends.
Compliance Note: Verified-origin sourcing maintained with rigorous documentation for international trade finance compliance.

Trading Principles

73 Energy operates as a principal trader. Our activities are governed by a robust 'Four-Eyes' internal risk framework. We do not participate in high-risk or sanctioned corridors. All transactions are executed under standardized international contracts (e.g., GAFTA, FOSFA, Incoterms 2020) and supported by structured trade finance (LC/SBLC/DLC).
