About 73 Energy

The Architects of Supply

Operating at the intersection of maritime logistics, structured trade finance, and regulatory governance, we execute back-to-back physical transactions backed by institutional credit facilities.

As principals in every transaction, we take title to commodities and assume the associated risks, differentiating ourselves from brokers and intermediaries.

The Origin of "73"

Our name is derived from the universal language of the sea. In the era of maritime telegraphy, "73" was the standard wire code for "Best Regards" — a signal of professional courtesy and mutual respect sent between officers across the open ocean.

Today, 73 Energy applies that same ethos to modern energy trading. In a market often clouded by noise and volatility, we act as the clear signal. We prioritize precision, integrity, and the maintenance of strong professional channels between buyers, suppliers, and regulatory bodies.
