About 73 Energy

Operating at the intersection of maritime logistics, structured trade finance, and regulatory governance, we execute back-to-back physical transactions backed by institutional credit facilities.

As principals in every transaction, we take title to commodities and assume the associated risks, differentiating ourselves from brokers and intermediaries. Our approach combines institutional-grade infrastructure with the agility required in today's dynamic commodity markets.
